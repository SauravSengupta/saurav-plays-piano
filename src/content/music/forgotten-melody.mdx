## The Story

**Forgotten Melody** got its name from a friend who heard it and loved it - but couldn't quite remember the tune afterward. That feeling of something just out of reach became the heart of the track.

Layered piano and soft synth pads at 90 BPM, with a quiet structural game underneath: a three-chord progression phrased so the resolution keeps shifting, never landing quite where you expect. Attentive listeners will catch it; everyone else will just find it airy and a little elusive - like a melody you're almost remembering.

*Thanks for listening,* - Saurav
